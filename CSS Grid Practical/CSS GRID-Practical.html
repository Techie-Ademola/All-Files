Our Vision To achieve excellence in Health manpower training and secure credibility in all aspects of our endeavour and thus become the best College of Health Technology with qualified staff, offering diverse programmes with full accreditation, and graduating students armed with knowledge, skill and the right attitude to face the challenges of Health care delivery system thus, making their horizon limitless. Our Mission To create, preserve and communicate knowledge in Health Science, Technology, behavioural and Management Sciences and scholarship in other fields of human endeavour and thereby contribute to the complete enhancement of health and well being of the people of Ogun State in particular, Nigeria and the entire globe.
